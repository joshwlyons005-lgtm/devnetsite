Pipeline

Content Pipeline

Submit, review, feature, how projects move through DevNet.

~/devnet/pipeline · runbook
  1. 01

    Submit

    Submit your project through the feature form. Doesn’t have to be a perfect build, an interesting idea with a working prototype is enough.

  2. 02

    Review

    Every submission is reviewed manually, no scoring, no algorithm, no engagement metrics. We look for working projects: a prototype with a working backend, something in beta/testing, or a published/live product.

  3. 03

    Feature

    Accepted projects are featured on DevNet’s X and LinkedIn, and you’re plugged into the network of devs.
