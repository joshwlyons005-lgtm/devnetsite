01 · Mission

What is DevNet?

DevNet is a network of devs spanning 13 hubs across North America — 5 in the US, 8 in Canada. We showcase builds, connect devs, and turn working projects into public proof of work.

02 · Stance

Our stance

Execution beats ideas. Real products beat concepts. Builders grow faster around other builders — so that’s what we wire up.

Pipeline

Content Pipeline

Submit, review, feature — how projects move through DevNet.

~/devnet/pipeline · runbook
  1. 01

    Submit

    Apply to have your project featured through the feature form. Prototype with a working backend, beta, or live product — all welcome.

  2. 02

    Review

    Every submission is reviewed manually. No scoring, no algorithm, no engagement metrics — just eyes on a working project.

  3. 03

    Feature

    Accepted projects are featured on DevNet’s X and LinkedIn, and you’re plugged into the network of devs.
